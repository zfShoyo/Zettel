# Cauchy-Schwarz
Assuming x and y are vectors in R^{N}, $$ |x \cdot y| \leq ||x|| * ||y||$$Additional:

Because of law of cosines where $$x \cdot y = ||x|| * ||y|| cos\theta$$
we have that $$||x|| * ||y||cos\theta \leq ||x|| * ||y||$$
Cancelling out the ||x|| * ||y|| we have $$cos\theta \leq 1$$
