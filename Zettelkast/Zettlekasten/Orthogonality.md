# Orthogonality
 We say that **u** and **v** are **orthogonal** if **u** * **v** = 0. (if the dot product is 0)

### To prove orthogonality, use
(Pythagorean Theorem) $u \cdot v = 0$ if and only if $||u||^2 + ||v||^2 = ||u+v||^2$
