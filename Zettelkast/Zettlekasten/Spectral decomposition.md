# Spectral decomposition
### Theorem
Let A be a nxn matrix. Given orthonormal eigenvectors $q_{1}, q_{2}, ... q_{3}$ corresponding to eigenvalues $e_1, e_2, ... e_n,$ $$A = e_1q_1q_1^T + e_2q_2q_2^T + ... + e_nq_nq_n^T$$
